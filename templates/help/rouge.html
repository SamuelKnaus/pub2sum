ROUGE

The ROUGE Python package from Google Research is used for this project. It is designed to duplicate the perl package from Lin (2004). ROUGE-1 and ROUGE-L metrics are implemented. Porter stemming is applied before the metrics are calculated.

  1. Copy and paste your reference summary in the field "Reference Summary".

  2. Copy and paste your generated summary in the field "Generated Summary".

  3. Click on Evaluate to calculate ROUGE scores.
