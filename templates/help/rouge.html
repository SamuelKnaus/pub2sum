ROUGE

The Python implementation of ROUGE from the Google Research team is used for this project. It is designed to duplicate the perl package from Lin (2004). The following metrics are implemented.

  • ROUGE-1
  • ROUGE-L

Porter stemming is applied before the metrics are calculated.
