Getting Started

Find out how  pub2sum can be employed to automatically generate human-like summaries for your scientific papers and generate copy-paste references on the go.

  1. Navigate to the Summarizer page.

  2. Click on Choose file.

  3. Select a scientific PDF paper from your device.

  4. Chose if you want  pub2sum to summarize and/or fetch references for you,

  5. Click on Summarize to start analyzing.

  6. The algorithm will now process your request automatically. This might take some time. Once done the site will refresh and show you the results depending on the options chose before.

After that you can easily copy & paste the results to your own document.
